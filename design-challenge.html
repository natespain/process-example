Design Challenge

First, we started with determining the design challenge. This is a worksheet from the Human Centered Design Toolkit helps frame the challenge as an actionable question.

What is the problen you're trying to solve?

How does the GCHC website contribute to the end of homelessness in Cincinnati?

Now, state the ultimate impact you're trying to have.

Increaesed support and donations to help eradicate homelessness in Cincinnati.

What are some possible solutions to your problem?

Hierarchy, Specific Goals shown on the homepage, appeal to main audience to support and volunteer, and a more approachable design.

Finally, write down some of the content and constraints that you're facing.

Only the Cincinnati, Ohio area. small organization, and I do not live inCincinnati to directly get information from them.

Does your original question need a tweak? Try it again.

How do I create a more specific goal, audience, and inviting homepage for the Cincinnati population to help eradicate homelessness.

This exercise was helpful but I realized that I needed to frame the question so that it included the ways the site listed to eradicate homelessness.

A better design challenge would be, "How do I create a website for GCCH that facilitates coordinating of services, educating the public, and engaging in advocacy to end homelessness in the Cincinnati area?"
